The Vision

By Latrice Banks

At the beginning of every year I always make a vision board. My main focus this year was going back to college in the summer, learn, and implement.

My goal is to make next summer my last semester at Oakland Community College so I can graduate and walk across the stage. I will be transferring to a university to become a software engineer. Ultimately, I want to work remote for Ford Motor Company as a Software Engineer. My entire family worked for the big three and I want to carry on that lagacy. Currently, I work remotely as a Logistics Coordinator. Working remotely has been a blessing for me because I am a single mother. It allows me to be there for my children and not have to rely on anyone.
